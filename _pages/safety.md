## Organizations {#organizations}

Orgs that are hiring, take collaborators, or run open communities.

### Research labs & nonprofits

- [Apollo Research](https://www.apolloresearch.ai/) — Researches and detects AI "scheming"; hiring across science/monitoring roles, and open to anyone with agent logs to analyze.
- [METR](https://metr.org/) — Measures whether frontier models can cause catastrophic harm; hiring technical staff plus an open expression-of-interest.
- [Redwood Research](https://www.redwoodresearch.org/) — AI control and threat-mitigation research; hires technical staff and points newcomers to MATS / Astra.
- [FAR.AI](https://far.ai/) — Research + field-building nonprofit; careers, the FAR.Labs Berkeley coworking space, and grants for independent researchers.
- [ARC](https://alignment.org/) — Theoretical alignment (formal explanations of model behavior); small, hires through its careers page.
- [MIRI](https://intelligence.org/) — Agent foundations and technical governance; maintains an open job board and welcomes unsolicited talent.
- [Transluce](https://transluce.org/) — Open interpretability and monitoring tooling; hiring plus external research collaborations.
- [Timaeus](https://timaeus.co/) — Interpretability via singular learning theory; hiring, a research-fellows program, Discord, and events.
- [Simplex](https://www.simplexaisafety.com/) — Theory of how neural nets organize representations; hiring research scientists in the Bay Area and London.
- [Goodfire](https://www.goodfire.ai/) — Interpretability lab (Ember/Silico tools); hiring, a Discord, and a research-access program.
- [Softmax](https://www.softmax.com/) — Multi-agent "organic alignment"; hiring, a Discord, and the open-source Cogames benchmark you can build on.
- [Decode Research](https://www.decoderesearch.org/) — Interpretability infrastructure (Neuronpedia, SAELens); open to partners and open-source contributors.
- [MIRI Technical Governance Team](https://techgov.intelligence.org/) — Technical analysis to inform AI governance; careers page plus an open invite for collaborations.
- [Poseidon Research](https://poseidonresearch.org/) — How AI systems represent and conceal information; open contact and an opportunities mailing list.
- [Luthien](https://luthien.cc/) — Open-source runtime-control proxy that enforces rules on AI agents; beta program and OSS contribution.
- [PRISM Eval](https://www.prism-eval.ai/) — Automated LLM red-teaming (BET) and a robustness leaderboard; closed beta and an open-source tool.
- [Theorem Labs](https://theoremlabs.com/) — AI systems that verify programs and write code; hiring via its careers page.
- [Epoch AI](https://epoch.ai/) — Measures and forecasts trends in AI capabilities, compute, and data; hiring researchers/data scientists and publishes open datasets.
- [AI Futures Project](https://www.aifutures.org/) — Forecasts the trajectory of advanced AI through scenario modeling (authors of "AI 2027"); open positions.
- [Forethought](https://www.forethought.org/) — Researches how society can prepare for advanced AI; hiring researchers, with visa sponsorship and remote options.
- [Andon Labs](https://andonlabs.com/) — Agent-control evals for "safe autonomous organizations," running real-world agent evals for major labs; hiring via its Join the Lab page.
- [Aintelope](https://www.aintelope.net/) — Biologically-inspired AGI alignment and AI-welfare research; remote-first roles via open application.
- [Fathom](https://fathom.org/) — AI-governance nonprofit building independent verification organizations; hiring across tech-governance roles.
- [Metaculus](https://www.metaculus.com/) — Forecasting platform building epistemic infrastructure; anyone can join and forecast, plus open tournaments and hiring.

### Academic labs

- [UC Berkeley CHAI](https://humancompatible.ai/) — Provably beneficial AI; open research-fellowship and research-collaborator tracks, plus a recurring internship.
- [Mila — Responsible AI](https://mila.quebec/en/research/strategic-priorities/responsible-ai) — Trustworthy/safe ML; internships, fellowships, PhD supervision, and a summer school.

### Safety-as-a-service & security companies

- [Gray Swan](https://www.grayswan.ai/) — Adversarial red-teaming; runs the public Gray Swan Arena competitions (cash prizes, anyone can enter) and hires.
- [Lakera](https://www.lakera.ai/) — GenAI security; runs the *Gandalf* hacking game (1M+ players) and a 1,000+-member Slack community.
- [Haize Labs](https://www.haizelabs.com/) — Automated red-teaming / reliability platform; hiring.
- [Irregular](https://irregular.com/) — Frontier AI security research (formerly Pattern Labs); hiring across research, engineering, and policy.
- [Atla](https://www.atla-ai.com/) — LLM-as-judge evaluation models; hiring, including a research internship.
- [AE Studio](https://ae.studio/ai-alignment) — In-house "neglected approaches" alignment team; hiring plus an open research-grants program.
- [Elicit](https://elicit.com/) — AI research assistant for evidence synthesis (public-benefit corp); hiring, and a free product to use.
- [SecureBio](https://securebio.org/) — Biosecurity + AI×bio model evaluations; hiring plus an AI×Biosecurity summer fellowship.
- [AVERI](https://www.averi.org/) — Advocates for third-party auditing of frontier AI; open invitation to researchers and policymakers.
- [AIUC](https://aiuc.com/) — Certification + insurance for AI agents (the AIUC-1 standard); auditor-accreditation and technical-contributor paths.
- [Heron](https://www.heronsec.ai/) — Pipelines cybersecurity pros into frontier AI security; a research fellowship, seminar community, and 1:1 advising.
- [Ulyssean](https://ulyssean.com/) — Hardware + software to secure the clusters where frontier AI is trained and deployed; hiring founding technical staff.
- [TamperSec](https://tampersec.com/) — Tamper-detection enclosures for AI chips (prevents weight extraction, supports compute governance); hiring engineering and BD roles.
- [Lucid Computing](https://lucidcomputing.ai/) — Verifiable/confidential compute issuing cryptographic "AI Passports" for auditable deployment; hiring.
- [Virtue AI](https://www.virtueai.com/) — AI security and compliance: continuous red-teaming, runtime guardrails, and governance for agents; hiring.
- [Asymmetric Security](https://www.asymmetricsecurity.com/) — AI agents for cyber incident response (DFIR); hiring incident-response roles in London and SF.
- [Gladstone AI](https://www.gladstone.ai/) — AI national-security research and risk briefings; runs a public "Foundations of AI" course and hires.

## Programs & Fellowships {#programs}

Cohort-based on-ramps to skill up and do real safety work.

- [MATS](https://www.matsprogram.org/) — 10-week mentored alignment-research fellowship in Berkeley/London; broad eligibility, recurring open cohorts.
- [SPAR](https://sparai.org/) — The largest part-time, remote mentored research program (run by Kairos) — ideal if you can't relocate.
- [ARENA](https://www.arena.education/) — In-person technical alignment coding bootcamp at LISA London; 2–3 cohorts/year, expression-of-interest between them.
- [BlueDot Impact](https://bluedot.org/) — Free AI-safety courses; the intro is self-paced and open, deeper courses run in cohorts.
- [Apart Research](https://apartresearch.com/) — Open monthly weekend research sprints, plus a fellowship that turns sprint results into published work.
- [Constellation programs](https://www.constellation.org/) — Berkeley research center; the Astra Fellowship, Visiting Fellowship, and Generator Residency (all funded).
- [GovAI fellowships](https://www.governance.ai/) — Summer/Winter research and applied fellowships in AI governance (cyclical).
- [CHAI programs](https://humancompatible.ai/jobs) — Research fellowship, research-collaborator track, and internships at Berkeley.
- [Anthropic Fellows](https://www.anthropic.com/) — Paid ~4–6 month external AI-safety research with mentorship and compute; recurring cohorts.
- [OpenAI Residency](https://openai.com/residency/) — 6-month residency for people pivoting into AI from math/physics/neuroscience; plus the Red Teaming Network.
- [SecureBio AI×Biosecurity Fellowship](https://securebio.org/) — Summer fellowship at the AI×bio intersection (with CBAI): mentors, workshops, talks.
- [Heron Research Fellowship](https://www.heronsec.ai/) — Onboarding into AI security via real projects and a supportive community.
- [Principles of Intelligence](https://princint.ai/) — Formerly PIBBSS; bridges complex-systems science and alignment via a 3-month summer fellowship and a paid math-alignment residency (with Iliad) at LISA London.
- [ERA Fellowship](https://erafellowship.org/) — 10-week fully-funded Cambridge fellowship for researchers/entrepreneurs working on frontier AI risk; expression of interest open.
- [AI Leadership Collective](https://aileadershipcollective.com/) — Free 3-month fellowship + community helping frontier-lab employees drive internal safety initiatives.

## Incubators & Coworking {#incubators}

For starting an org, finding cofounders, or just being in the room.

- [Catalyze Impact](https://www.catalyze-impact.org/) — Nonprofit incubator for new AI-safety orgs; cofounder matching, mentoring, and funding intros.
- [Seldon Lab](https://seldonlab.com/) — Accelerator for "existential security" startups; runs cohort batches.
- [Future of Life Foundation](https://www.flf.org/) — Helps spin up 3–5 new orgs/year via a fellowship and incubation.
- [Constellation Incubator](https://www.constellation.org/programs/constellation-incubator) — Berkeley office space and support for full-time AI-safety founders; rolling admissions.
- [Entrepreneur First — def/acc](https://www.joinef.com/) — Talent investor; the def/acc cohort helps individuals find cofounders in defensive tech.
- [Y Combinator](https://www.ycombinator.com/) — General accelerator with rolling batches; safety-relevant companies welcome (check the RFS).
- [Fifty Years — 5050 AI](https://www.fiftyyears.com/5050/ai) — 13-week program turning scientists/engineers into safe-AI founders (annual cohorts).
- [Halcyon Futures](https://halcyonfutures.org/) — Funds + incubates founders across AI safety/bio/cyber; rolling "request for founders." *(also a funder, below)*
- [Antientropy — Sparkwell](https://www.antientropy.org/sparkwell) — Fiscal sponsorship + acceleration for nonprofit GCR projects; rolling admissions.
- [LISA](https://www.safeai.org.uk/) — London coworking hub with memberships, residencies, and hosted programs; rolling applications.
- [Mox](https://moxsf.com/) — SF coworking + community for safety-minded builders; day passes or membership.
- [AI Safety Founders Network](https://aisfounders.com/) — Community connecting people building for-profit AI-safety startups; cofounder matching, Discord, and events.

*Fiscal sponsors* (back-office homes for new nonprofit projects): [Ashgro](https://www.ashgro.org/), [Rethink Priorities Special Projects](https://rethinkpriorities.org/our-services/special-projects/), [EV Operations](https://ev.org/ops/), [Impact Ops](https://impact-ops.org/), [Berkeley Existential Risk Initiative](https://existence.org/), [Players Philanthropy Fund](https://www.ppf.org/fiscal-sponsorship).

## Funding {#funding}

Funders with a real application path — for-profit and philanthropic.

### VC funds

- [Safe AI Fund (SAIF)](https://www.saif.vc/) — Early-stage; $100K + mentorship for AI safety/security startups; open "Request Funding" form.
- [Anthology Fund (Menlo × Anthropic)](https://menlovc.com/anthology-fund/) — Pre-seed–Series A incl. trust & safety tooling; rolling application + Claude credits.
- [Halcyon Futures](https://halcyonfutures.org/) — Grants, investment, and incubation for AI safety/bio/cyber founders; rolling and fast.
- [Fifty Years — 5050 AI](https://www.fiftyyears.com/5050/ai) — Founder program + investment for safe-AI startups; publishes an AI-safety RFS.
- [Mythos Ventures](https://www.mythos.vc/) — Pre-seed/seed for founders building safe AI systems; warm intro (no public form).
- [Lionheart Ventures](https://www.lionheart.vc/) — Seed VC mitigating civilizational risk via transformative AI; pitch by email/intro.
- [Macroscopic Ventures](https://macroscopic.org/) — Grants + investments with a long-term AI-risk focus (deprioritizes unsolicited proposals).
- [Babushkin Ventures](https://babuschk.in/) — Large new AI-safety fund (Igor Babuschkin); direct outreach.
- [Auspicious Fund](https://auspiciousfund.com/) — Invests in AI for human flourishing; contact to explore.
- [Juniper Ventures](https://juniperventures.xyz/) — Pre-seed/seed VC backing founders making AI "secure and beneficial" (portfolio incl. Goodfire, Apollo, Softmax); open to pitches.

### Grant funders

- [Coefficient Giving](https://www.coefficientgiving.org/) — Formerly Open Philanthropy; capacity-building + technical AI safety + governance RFPs with open windows.
- [Survival and Flourishing Fund](https://survivalandflourishing.fund/) — Funds long-term survival/flourishing orgs; annual round plus a rolling form.
- [Long-Term Future Fund](https://funds.effectivealtruism.org/funds/far-future) — Rolling grants for AI safety / x-risk, from individuals to orgs.
- [Manifund](https://manifund.org/) — Open funding platform: post a project, regrantors fund quickly; can fiscally sponsor individuals.
- [Foresight Institute](https://foresight.org/grants/grants-ai-for-science-safety/) — AI safety/science grants ($10K–$100K), rolling monthly review, plus the Foresight Fellowship.
- [Future of Life Institute](https://futureoflife.org/) — Periodic fellowships, RFPs, and contests (no unsolicited proposals — watch for rounds).
- [AI Risk Mitigation Fund](https://www.airiskfund.com/) — LTFF spinout for technical alignment, policy, and capacity (grant announcements pending).
- [AI Safety Tactical Opportunities Fund](https://manifund.org/JueYan) — Fast pooled funding via Manifund; opportunity-sourced.
- [Institute for Progress — The Launch Sequence](https://ifp.org/rfp-launch/) — Rolling RFP for projects preparing the world for advanced AI; honorarium for published plans.
- [AI Alignment Foundation](https://www.aialignmentfoundation.org/) — Rolling grants for original alignment research, prioritizing originality over credentials.
- [AE Studio Research Grants](https://research.ae.studio/) — Funds bold ideas to advance AI alignment; open submission.
- [CSET Foundational Research Grants](https://cset.georgetown.edu/foundational-research-grants/) — Georgetown calls funding external technical research on frontier-AI risk with policy relevance.
- [ARIA — Safeguarded AI](https://www.aria.org.uk/opportunity-spaces/mathematics-for-safe-ai/safeguarded-ai/) — UK agency funding formal-methods / high-assurance approaches to safe AI; open calls + rolling seeds.
- [Cooperative AI Foundation](https://www.cooperativeai.com/foundation) — Research grants and PhD fellowships on cooperative AI.
- [Center on Long-Term Risk Fund](https://longtermrisk.org/grantmaking/) — Rolling grants for work on suffering-risks (s-risks).
- [BlueDot grants](https://bluedot.org/programs/rapid-grants) — Fast micro-grants ($50–$10k) and career-transition grants to go full-time on AI safety.
- [EA Infrastructure Fund](https://funds.effectivealtruism.org/funds/ea-community) — Rolling grants for field-building and community infrastructure.
- [Superlinear](https://www.super-linear.org/) — Cash prizes and bounties for AI-safety challenges; open submissions while a prize is posted.
- [Nonlinear Network](https://www.nonlinear.org/network.html) — One application reaches 50+ AI-safety funders.
- [GiveWiki](https://givewiki.org/) — Publish a funding-constrained project to attract donors and regrantors.

## Conferences & Events {#events}

Where to meet people and find collaborators.

- [FAR.AI Alignment Workshop](https://www.far.ai/events/alignment-workshop) — Curated ~300-person technical alignment gathering, several times a year near major ML conferences; apply via EOI.
- [EA Global](https://www.effectivealtruism.org/ea-global) — Multi-city conferences with a heavy AI-safety track, built around 1:1 networking; application-based.
- [The Curve](https://thecurve.goldengateinstitute.org/) — Curated big-picture AI conference at Lighthaven, Berkeley; annual, application/invite.
- [ILIAD](https://www.iliadconference.com/) — Free alignment unconference with participant-led sessions, Berkeley; annual, open application.
- [Apart Research hackathons](https://apartresearch.com/sprints) — Monthly open weekend research sprints, remote + global hubs; free, no credentials needed.
- [IASEAI](https://www.iaseai.org/) — Academic/policy conference of the Intl. Association for Safe & Ethical AI; annual, call for papers.
- [TAIS](https://tais2026.cc/) — One-day technical AI-safety conference, Oxford; open registration.
- [ControlConf](https://controlconf.org/) — Conference on AI control — keeping powerful AI systems in check; gathers researchers working on control techniques.

*Also:* safety workshops co-located with NeurIPS/ICML/ICLR (tracker: [mlsafety.org/events](https://www.mlsafety.org/events)), and the online [MAISU](https://www.lesswrong.com/events) unconference.
