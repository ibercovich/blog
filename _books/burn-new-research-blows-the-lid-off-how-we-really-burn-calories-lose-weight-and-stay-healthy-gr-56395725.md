synopsis: Burn examines how the human body actually regulates energy expenditure, challenging popular assumptions that exercise directly increases calorie burn or that metabolism varies widely between individuals. Drawing on fieldwork with the Hadza hunter-gatherers of Tanzania, evolutionary anthropologist Herman Pontzer shows that daily calorie expenditure falls within a constrained range regardless of activity level, and explains the implications for weight management and overall health.
